Role
Top Graphics Editor


As the Top Editor of Graphics, I am responsible for the entire graphics section for both the print and online versions of the Bruin. I oversee the work of 13 contributors and supervise the scheduling of all standalone content. I organize the team and lead meetings where we ideate and assign standalone pieces. I provide ongoing technical and design training for the team and work closely with other top editors on our print issue everyday. I have also been integral in establishing the best collaborative processes between departments for improved work flow and faster turn around times. Being a daily publication, meeting immediate deadlines and adapting to changing requirements are essential, and I learned quickly how to think on my feet.

Changing Habits

Role
Layout design, ideation, brand creation, production and sewing


This project is two books I produced for JUNK BATTLE happening in April of 2018, as well as a brand and product I created. Here I ask the question, what can we do with all the trash we already have, and what can we do in the future to produce our waste production?

The First Book

The first book, called "Becoming Aware," cronicles a week of all the waste I produced and includes my thoughts and illustrations of what I got out of the experience. I designed the book layout and in the spirit of waste reduction, all the paper is recycled as well as the covers.

My Music Maker

Role
Creator, Designer, Programer


My Music Maker was inspired by my frustration with complicated music creation systems. I wanted to create an application that would be easy and intuitive for anyone to use. With this tablet app, people can make simple songs easily.

Play below!


This is a coding project done using the javascript library p5js. It is an app that enables anyone to create music very easily. There are three types of sounds: beats, percussion, and melody. Begin building your song by clicking on an instrument, then use your keyboard or click on the screen to add notes and chords.

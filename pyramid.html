The Illuminati Challenge

Role
Creator, Designer, Fabrication


This is a wearable maze game where the player tilts their head according to directions given by another player, with the goal of getting a ball through the maze.

How It Works

The second player, not wearing the maze first drops the ball into the top of the maze. Both of the players goal is to manuver the ball so that it falls out the back of the pyramid. The front of the pyramid includes a space for the user to attach their phone and try to solve the phone maze at the same time as trying to solve the pyramid maze. The hat is Illuminati themed because of the way two people interact with each other while playing: The person inside the hat has to follow every step the person on the outside tells them to do, with no freewill for themselves. They must trust that their partner outside is telling them the right moves in order to beat the game.
